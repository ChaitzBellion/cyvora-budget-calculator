My Story

I believe that financial literacy is one of the most important life skills that is rarely taught in schools. After spending years learning about personal finance, investing, and wealth building, I realized that this knowledge should be accessible to everyone.

WealthPilot was born from this belief. It started as a simple budget calculator I built for myself, and grew into a comprehensive platform with tools, tips, and in-depth content to help others on their financial journey.

My goal is to demystify personal finance and make it approachable for everyone, whether you are just starting your career or looking to optimize your existing financial strategy.
