About the Creator

Hi, I'm Suman Chaitanya

Associate, Google Cloud Certified professional, and creator of Cyvora—a platform where I share practical AI tools, cloud computing knowledge, software development tutorials, productivity resources, and career guidance to help people learn by building.

My Story

Technology has always fascinated me—not just because of what it can do today, but because of what it makes possible tomorrow.

While pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering, I developed a strong interest in software, automation, cloud technologies and solving real-world problems through technology. That curiosity eventually led me to begin my career at Cognizant, where I work across multiple business functions including IT Service Management, process improvement, Power BI reporting, automation, networking and enterprise support.

Working with global clients and leadership teams taught me something important: technical knowledge alone isn't enough. The people who create the biggest impact continuously learn, adapt and build.

That mindset inspired me to create Cyvora.

Cyvora started as a personal project where I could document everything I was learning—from AI tools and cloud computing to Python, SQL, data engineering and web development. Instead of keeping notes for myself, I wanted to build practical tools, write tutorials and create resources that anyone could use.

Today, Cyvora is my learning playground. Every article, project and tool represents something I've learned, experimented with or built myself.

My long-term vision is to transform Cyvora into a SaaS platform that provides practical tools and educational resources for students, developers and professionals around the world.
