Paths

Career Paths Explained

Understand different career trajectories in IT and related fields.

Backend Development

Focus on server-side logic, databases, and APIs. Learn Node.js, Python, Java, and gain expertise in system design and scalability.

Data Analytics

Master SQL, Python, Power BI, and Tableau. Learn data visualization and business intelligence to drive insights and decisions.

Automation Testing

Learn Selenium, API testing, and CI/CD pipelines. Develop expertise in quality assurance and test automation frameworks.

Cloud Engineering

Get certified in AWS, Azure, or GCP. Learn infrastructure, deployment, and DevOps to manage scalable cloud applications.

Full Stack Development

Master both frontend and backend. Learn React, Node.js, databases, and build complete end-to-end applications.

AI/ML Engineering

Learn Python, TensorFlow, and machine learning algorithms. Build AI models and work on cutting-edge AI applications.
